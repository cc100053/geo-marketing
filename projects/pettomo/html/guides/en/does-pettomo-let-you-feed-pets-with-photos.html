PetTomo has streamlined the photo feeding process to be intuitive and rewarding, ensuring that integrating real-world visuals into your virtual pet's life is seamless. Here’s a breakdown of the typical photo feeding workflow within PetTomo:

  1. Initiate the Feed Action: Within your shared room, you'll select the designated "feed" option, signaling your intention to nourish your virtual pet.
  2. Capture or Select a Photo: You then have the choice to either take a new photo using your device's camera in real-time or select an existing image from your photo gallery. This flexibility allows for spontaneity or curated memory sharing.
  3. Image Upload and Validation: Once a photo is chosen, PetTomo uploads the image. During this stage, the app performs a validation check. This ensures that the image meets the app's standards and technical specifications (e.g., file type, size).

  6. Potential Rewards: Depending on the specific action or context, users may also be granted in-app rewards, such as "Candy," adding an extra layer of incentive and progress to the interaction.

This detailed process ensures that each photo feed is a deliberate, interactive step that contributes positively to the virtual pet's well-being and the collective memory of the shared room.
