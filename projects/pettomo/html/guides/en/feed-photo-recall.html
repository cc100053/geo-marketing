Can you recall a feed photo in PetTomo?

PetTomo 2.0.1 adds feed-photo recall. Learn how to think about sent photos, shared-room visibility, and what stays after a recalled feed photo.

Updated May 31, 2026

PetTomo lets invited room members feed a shared virtual pet with photos. In the 2.0.1 local App Store metadata and in-app What's New copy, PetTomo describes a new ability to recall sent feed photos, plus smoother avatar and photo display.

That makes feed photos easier to manage after sending. PetTomo remains a private shared-room app: feed photos, chat, memories, and pet care happen inside the room for active members, not in a public social feed.

Key takeaways

  • PetTomo feed photos are part of a private invited room.
  • Version 2.0.1 local release copy says sent feed photos can be recalled.
  • App text says recalling a feed photo removes the photo for everyone.
  • The confirmation copy says coins and the pet's meal are kept after recall.
  • Recall is useful for accidental or wrong-photo sends, but it is not a substitute for checking before sharing.

Why feed-photo recall matters

Photo feeding is one of PetTomo's signature actions. A lunch photo, a walk, a pet photo, or a small daily scene can become part of the shared pet routine. Because photos feel personal, users need a clear way to handle mistakes.

Recall helps with simple cases: choosing the wrong image, sending too quickly, or deciding a photo should not stay visible in the shared room.

What changes after recall

The app localization copy for the recall confirmation explains that the photo will be removed for everyone, while coins and the pet's meal are kept. That wording is important because it separates the photo from the completed pet-care action.

In practical terms, describe recall as photo visibility cleanup. Do not describe it as undoing every room event, rewinding chat history, or changing all rewards.

Good habits before sending feed photos

Check the room

Make sure you are in the intended PetTomo room. Many users share rooms with partners, friends, or small groups, so the right room matters.

Check the image

Choose a photo that you are comfortable sharing with active members of that room. PetTomo is private, but the room is still shared.

Use recall for mistakes

If you send the wrong feed photo, use recall where available. Then send the intended photo if you still want to feed the pet with a different moment.

FAQ

Is PetTomo feed-photo recall public?

No. PetTomo is centered on private shared rooms joined by invite link or invite code.

Does recall remove the photo for everyone?

The app's recall confirmation copy says the photo will be removed for everyone.

Does recalling a feed photo remove the pet's meal?

The app copy says coins and the pet's meal are kept after the photo is recalled.

Summary

PetTomo feed-photo recall is a practical privacy and correction feature for shared pet rooms. It helps members clean up accidental photo sends while keeping the shared pet-care routine lightweight and private.
